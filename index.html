Not just a café —

carry long after you leave.

Tucked in the heart of Ahmedabad, Fresh Bite was born from a single belief — that great coffee and carefully prepared food deserve a space as considered as the experience itself.

We source our beans with discipline, cook with restraint, and plate with purpose. At Fresh Bite, slowing down is the whole point.

The Mind Behind Fresh Bite

Arjun Shah

Founder & Head of Experience

Arjun grew up watching his grandmother run a small tea stall near the old city. He went on to train as a barista in Chikmagalur and spent two years working alongside chefs in Bangalore and Goa. He returned to Ahmedabad with one clear intention: build the café he always wanted to find but never could.

Fresh Bite is that café. Every decision — from the bean sourcing to the chair selection — passes through a single question: does this make the guest feel something?

— Arjun
